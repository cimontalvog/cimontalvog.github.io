Clara Montalvo

Clara Montalvo is the principal worker in the El Olivo’s kitchen, specializing in creating Spanish dishes with flavors which transport you to the Mediterranean coast, for example Galicia, located in the north of Spain. She has a deep motivation for traditional recipes. She is a professional in creating perfectly paellas and tasty tapas that makes her a very good chef using classic and methodologies techniques with fresh, local ingredients and with an unforgettable dining experience.

Alejandro Suárez

Alejandro Suárez is the deputy manager of the restaurant. He brings creative ideas to El Olivo’s menu, using a combination of traditional Spanish cuisine and a modern innovation at the present period of time. He’s well-known for his meticulous attention to detail, Alejandro is the one who makes stunning presentations and unique flavor combinations. From his signature tortilla de patata to the decadent desserts, Alejandro’s dishes are delicious, acclaiming him from regulars and newcomers alike.

David Lerena

David Lerena, the grill chef of El Olivo, is popular renowned for his ability to bring out the best in every meal. His abilities are based on grilling meats and seafood, creating dishes that are rich, smoky, and full of Spanish character. David’s passion for culinary precision to sourcing the finest ingredients ensure that every bite reflects the spirit of Spanish traditions.
