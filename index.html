History of El Olivo

El Olivo is a Spanish restaurant located in the heart of the center of Dublin. Since opening its doors in 2024, it has become a favourite place for lovers of Spanish cuisine. The restaurant was founded by a group of passionate chefs who wanted to bring the quality of Spain to Ireland. Over the years, El Olivo has built a strong reputation, friendly service, and delicious and tasty dishes such as paella, tapas, and freshly made churros. Whether you're looking for a relaxed meal with friends or a romantic dinner, El Olivo offers an unforgettable feeling of a Spain memory.
